Care Guides

Choose Your
Care Category

From lingerie to skincare — every piece of your BBW4LIFE collection deserves specific, loving attention.

Washing

  • Hand wash in cold water (30°C max)
  • Use a delicate mesh laundry bag
  • Gentle detergent — no bleach
  • Never use the dryer
  • No wringing — press gently
Turn inside out before washing to preserve colour and embroidery.

Drying

  • Air dry flat on a clean towel
  • Keep away from direct sunlight
  • Reshape cups while damp
  • No tumble drying — ever
  • Avoid hanging — distorts shape
Flat drying preserves the elasticity of underwire and lace panels.

Storage

  • Stack bras cup-in-cup
  • Store in a breathable drawer
  • Keep away from perfume sprays
  • Never fold underwire bras
  • Avoid plastic sealed bags
Use lavender sachets to keep lingerie fresh without chemicals.

Longevity

  • Rotate between 3–4 pieces
  • Wash after every 2–3 wears
  • Replace elastic every 6–12 months
  • Don't wear in pool / spa
  • Avoid sitting on rough surfaces
Good care = your lingerie lasts 3× longer. That's real value.

Washing

  • Follow label instructions always
  • Cold wash for dark colours
  • Similar colours together
  • No hot water for stretch fabrics
  • No harsh detergents
Cold wash reduces colour bleeding and protects stretch-fit fabrics.

Ironing

  • Use low heat on synthetic blends
  • Iron inside out
  • Use a pressing cloth for prints
  • No steam on embroidered areas
  • No iron on sequins or beading
A garment steamer is gentler than an iron for most plus-size cuts.

Storage

  • Hang dresses & blouses
  • Fold knits — don't hang
  • Use padded hangers for shoulders
  • No wire hangers
  • Avoid direct sunlight in wardrobe
Gravity pulls knitwear out of shape — always fold them flat.

Stain Removal

  • Act immediately — blot, don't rub
  • Cold water for protein stains
  • White vinegar for deodorant marks
  • No hot water on fresh stains
  • No bleach on colours
Baking soda + cold water = a gentle stain paste that works wonders.

Skincare

  • Moisturise after every shower
  • SPF daily — even indoors
  • Gentle exfoliation 2×/week
  • No harsh scrubs on sensitive areas
  • Avoid alcohol-based toners
Curves deserve hydration — massage lotion in upward circular motions.

Body Care

  • Use body oil on damp skin
  • Massage underarm & crease areas
  • pH-balanced intimate wash
  • No scented soaps on sensitive skin
  • Avoid very hot baths daily
Shea butter + jojoba oil = the ultimate plus-size skin duo.

Product Storage

  • Keep away from heat & direct light
  • Check expiry dates regularly
  • Clean applicators weekly
  • No bathroom shelf (humidity)
  • Don't share mascara or lip products
A cool, dark drawer is better for your skincare than any display shelf.

Self-Care Ritual

  • 5 minutes daily — body appreciation
  • Mirror affirmations — they work
  • Drink 2L water — skin loves it
  • No negative self-talk allowed
  • No skipping your night routine
Your body is not a problem to fix. It's a masterpiece to maintain.

Jewellery

  • Wipe after each wear
  • Store separately in pouches
  • Remove before shower & sport
  • No contact with perfume
  • No chlorine or sea water
A soft toothbrush + warm soapy water revives most jewellery instantly.

Bags & Belts

  • Stuff bags when storing
  • Wipe leather with conditioner
  • Air regularly — avoid mustiness
  • No direct sunlight storage
  • No plastic wrap — use dust bags
Fill bags with tissue paper to help them hold shape between uses.

Scarves & Wraps

  • Hand wash silks only
  • Roll — don't fold — for storage
  • Air dry in shade
  • No machine wash for silk
  • No direct ironing on print
Rolling prevents creases and keeps delicate fabrics in perfect shape.

Shoes & Heels

  • Clean after every use
  • Use shoe trees for shape
  • Waterproof spray on new shoes
  • No storing in original box long-term
  • No stacking heels
Wide-fit shoes need shoe trees to maintain arch support structure.

Label Guide

Decode Your
Care Labels

Those little symbols on your labels are your garment speaking to you. Here's what they mean.

Wash tub Machine washable. Number inside = max temp (°C)
Tub + line Machine wash — gentle / delicate cycle only
Circle crossed Do NOT dry clean
X in square Do NOT tumble dry
Iron symbol Dots = heat level. 1 dot = low, 3 dots = high
Hand in tub Hand wash only in cold/lukewarm water
Circle Dry clean — letter inside specifies solvent
Triangle Bleaching allowed. Crossed = no bleach at all

Care FAQ

We strongly recommend hand washing all lingerie pieces in cold water. If you must use a machine, always use a delicate mesh laundry bag on the gentlest cycle (30°C max). Never use the spin cycle or tumble dry.

Act fast — blot (never rub) with a clean white cloth. Use cold water for most stains. For stubborn marks, apply a small amount of gentle liquid detergent and let sit for 5 minutes before rinsing. Avoid hot water which sets stains permanently.

With proper hand washing, flat drying, and correct storage, quality lingerie can last 1–3 years. The key is rotating between pieces, never over-washing, and always following our care guides. Elastic degrades with heat — keep it away from the dryer.
