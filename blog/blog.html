✦ Let's Set the Record Straight

Myth vs. Fact

Common misconceptions — debunked by our experts.

Myth

"Carbs make you gain weight."

Fact

Consistently overeating calories causes weight gain. Nutrient-dense carbs are your body's preferred energy source — cutting them entirely often backfires.

Myth

"You need intense workouts to see results."

Fact

Gentle, consistent movement builds sustainable progress without burnout. 20 minutes daily beats one brutal session per week every time.

Myth

"Weight loss is about willpower alone."

Fact

It's a combination of sustainable habits, emotional support, and understanding your body's hormonal and metabolic needs — not just discipline.
