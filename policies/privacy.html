1. Who We Are

CurvaFit is a science-based weight loss platform founded on November 5, 2025 by Paul Francenel, designed specifically for plus-size women. We provide structured meal plans, low-impact home workouts, and community support to help women achieve safe, sustainable fat loss — without pills, crash diets, or extreme restriction.

CurvaFit does not deliver programs directly. We work in partnership with specialized fitness platforms that deliver the content to you by email once your program is chosen. Our website is www.curva-fit.com. When you interact with CurvaFit — visiting our site, purchasing a program, joining our community, or contacting our team — you share data with us. This policy tells you exactly how that data is handled.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used for exactly these purposes — and nothing else:

  • Program delivery: Forward your access to our partner platforms who deliver meal plans, workout videos, and guided content by email
  • Support: Read and respond to your questions, technical issues, or billing requests
  • Program emails: Send emails that are part of your program (weekly check-ins, plateau support) — these are not marketing, they are program content
  • Newsletter: Send tips and updates — only if you explicitly subscribed, always with a one-click unsubscribe link
  • Refund verification: In result-based refund cases, review the proof of use you voluntarily submit (photos, videos, or progress tracking over up to 15 days)
  • Site improvement: Analyze anonymized, aggregated usage data to improve content quality
  • Fraud prevention: Use IP addresses and purchase patterns to detect fraudulent transactions
  • Legal compliance: Retain purchase records for the legally required period (5 years in most jurisdictions)

5. Payment Processing

CurvaFit does not store, process, or have access to your credit card or bank account details. All payments are processed by Stripe and PayPal — two of the most trusted PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment processors in the world. We also accept all major credit and debit cards including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.

When you enter payment information, it goes directly to the secure servers of Stripe or PayPal depending on your chosen payment method. CurvaFit receives only a transaction confirmation and the last 4 digits of your card for your records. Your full card details, PayPal credentials, and banking information never pass through or are stored on CurvaFit systems.

Stripe privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy · PayPal privacy policy: paypal.com/privacy

Refund requests:

Standard refund: Products must be returned in original condition within a reasonable delay. Refunds processed within up to 30 days (may vary depending on our partners).

Result-based refund: Requires proof of regular use over up to 15 days (photos, videos, or progress tracking). Our team reviews your submission and processes the refund within up to 30 days if conditions are met.

Subscription cancellation: You may cancel at any time. Processing takes up to 10 days via our partner platforms. A partial refund for unused time may be issued via your original payment method — or another method if requested at least 5 days before processing begins.

Include in all requests: Your purchase email and order number for faster processing.

Essential cookies Keep you logged in, remember your cart, enable checkout — the site cannot function without these No (required for function)

Browser settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies. You can also install the Google Analytics Opt-Out Add-On. Note: disabling essential cookies will prevent login and checkout from working.

8. Data Security

We take the following concrete measures to protect your data:

  • SSL encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS/SSL. The padlock in your browser confirms this.
  • Secure hosting: Our servers are hosted with providers that maintain physical and digital security including firewalls, intrusion detection, and regular audits.
  • Access control: Only team members who need your data for their specific role can access it. A support agent does not have access to your billing history. A billing agent does not have access to your health data.
  • Password hashing: Passwords are never stored in plain text. They are hashed using one-way encryption — CurvaFit cannot see your password.
  • Health data isolation: Your weight, measurements, and progress data are stored separately from your account and billing data and are never used for marketing analysis.
  • Proof of use data: Photos, videos, or progress documentation submitted for refund requests are used solely for that evaluation and deleted once the request is processed.

No system is 100% secure. In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you and the relevant authorities within 72 hours as required by GDPR.

10. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Union, United Kingdom, or any jurisdiction with equivalent data protection laws, you have the following rights over your personal data. These rights are real, actionable, and free to exercise:

Right of Access

Request a copy of all personal data CurvaFit holds about you. We provide it in a readable format within 30 days.

Right of Rectification

Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete data we hold. We update it within 30 days.

Right to Erasure

Request complete deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten"). We delete all non-legally-required data within 30 days.

Right to Portability

Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format (CSV or JSON) so you can transfer it elsewhere.

Right to Object

Object to any specific use of your data — including marketing emails, analytics, or profiling. We honor your objection immediately.

Right to Restriction

Limit how we use your data without deleting it — for example, keep your account but stop all marketing use.

How to Exercise Your Rights

Email with your full name, the email address on your account, and a description of the right you wish to exercise. We confirm receipt within 48 hours and fulfill the request within 30 days.

If you believe we have not handled your data correctly, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority (e.g. ICO in the UK, CNIL in France, or the equivalent in your country).
