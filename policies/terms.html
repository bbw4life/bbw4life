10. Results Disclaimer

BBW4LIFE does not sell unrealistic promises. We do not claim results such as "lose 7 kg in 10 days." We believe in honest, sustainable transformation that respects your body.

If you apply our recommendations seriously and consistently, you have a strong chance — up to 70% — of achieving concrete results. However:

  • Results vary from person to person based on starting weight, adherence, sleep quality, stress levels, and individual medical conditions
  • The numbers shown in success stories represent members with complete and consistent adherence to the full program
  • Partial adherence will produce proportionally smaller results — this is biology, not a program failure
  • Safe fat loss is 0.5 to 1 kg per week — the medically recognized safe range. Any result faster than this is primarily water weight, not fat loss
  • BBW4LIFE recommends measuring progress over 4-week periods, not daily — the scale fluctuates naturally by 0.5–1 kg day to day
