1. Our Approach to Refunds

CurvaFit was founded on a simple idea: help real people get real results, honestly. That same honesty extends to our refund policy. We do not hide behind walls of legal text designed to discourage returns. If you have a legitimate concern, we want to hear it and resolve it fairly.

Because CurvaFit works in partnership with specialized fitness platforms and third-party product suppliers, some timelines and conditions depend on our partners. We are transparent about those dependencies in each section below.

For any refund or return request, contact our billing team directly at and include your purchase email and order number for faster processing.

2. Product Returns

CurvaFit's Shop offers a curated selection of fitness accessories and home training equipment sourced through trusted partner suppliers. Because these products are not manufactured by CurvaFit directly, returns are processed in collaboration with our partners.

Eligibility Conditions

To be eligible for a product return and refund, the following conditions must be met:

  • The product must be returned in its original condition — unused, undamaged, and in its original packaging
  • The return request must be submitted within a reasonable delay after reception (typically within 14 days — contact us if your situation is exceptional)
  • You must provide proof of purchase (order confirmation email or order number)

Return Timeline

Step Action Timeline
1 Submit your return request to As soon as possible after reception
2 Our team reviews your request and sends return instructions Within 5 business days
3 You return the item following the instructions provided Within the delay specified
4 Partner validates the return and triggers the refund Up to 30 days after validation

⚠️ Important: Before You Order

To avoid return complications, please take these steps before placing your order:

  • Check your size carefully — consult the size guide on the product page before ordering apparel or fitted equipment
  • Verify your delivery address — ensure your shipping address is complete and accurate before confirming your order

CurvaFit cannot be held responsible for delivery issues caused by an incorrect address provided at checkout.

3. Result-Based Refund

CurvaFit is committed to honest transformation. We do not promise miracles — we state clearly that if you apply our programs seriously, you have up to a 70% chance of achieving concrete results. We stand behind that claim.

If you have followed a CurvaFit program genuinely and have not seen results, you may be eligible for a result-based refund — provided the following conditions are met:

Regular Use

You must have used the product or followed the program consistently over the required period — not sporadically or once.

Proof of Use

You will be asked to provide documentation of your usage: photos, videos, or a progress log covering up to 15 days of consistent use.

Honest Sharing

We ask you to share your experience honestly — what you tried, what you felt, and why you believe the program did not work for you.

Team Review

Our team carefully reviews your submission. If conditions are verified, your refund is processed within up to 30 days.

How to Submit a Result-Based Refund Request

  1. Email with subject line: "Result-Based Refund Request"
  2. Include your full name, purchase email, and order number
  3. Attach your proof of use: photos, videos, or a written progress log covering up to 15 days
  4. Briefly describe your experience and why you feel results were not achieved
  5. Our team will confirm receipt within 48 hours and notify you of the outcome within 30 days

Note: The 15-day documentation period is the maximum required — not a waiting period. If you have existing documentation from your program period, you may submit it immediately. This process exists to ensure fairness for all members and to allow us to improve our programs based on real feedback.

4. Subscription Cancellation

At CurvaFit, we believe in freedom. You can cancel your subscription at any time — no hoops, no guilt. Here is exactly what happens when you do:

Step What Happens Timeline
1 You request cancellation via email to Any time
2 We initiate the cancellation with our partner platforms Within 2 business days
3 Full processing and confirmation of cancellation Up to 10 days (partner-dependent)
4 Unused subscription time is calculated from your cancellation date Upon confirmation
5 Partial refund issued for unused time (if applicable) Via original payment method

Partial Refund for Unused Time

If your current subscription period has not been fully used at the time of cancellation, we will calculate the remaining unused time and issue a proportional partial refund where applicable. This refund will be processed via your original payment method unless you request an alternative.

Changing Your Refund Payment Method

If you wish to receive your refund via a different payment method than the one used at the time of purchase, you must inform us at least 5 days before the refund process begins. Contact with your request. We cannot modify the payment destination once processing has started.

5. How Refunds Are Processed

All refunds at CurvaFit follow the same clear process:

  • Method: Refunds are returned via the same payment method used at the time of purchase (Stripe, PayPal, Visa, Mastercard, American Express, etc.) — unless you request an alternative at least 5 days before processing
  • Timeline: Up to 30 days from validation — this window accounts for processing time on our end and with our payment partners
  • Currency: Refunds are issued in the same currency as the original transaction
  • Notification: You will receive an email confirmation once your refund has been initiated
  • Bank delay: After we initiate the refund, your bank or payment provider may take an additional 3–10 business days to reflect the amount in your account — this is outside our control

Always include in your refund request: Your purchase email address and your order number. This allows our team to process your request without delays or back-and-forth.

Refund email:

Response time: We confirm all refund requests within 48 hours and resolve them within 30 days.

6. Non-Refundable Situations

We are committed to fairness — for you and for all CurvaFit members. The following situations are generally not eligible for a refund:

  • Products that have been used, damaged, or returned without original packaging
  • Digital program content that has already been fully accessed and delivered
  • Refund requests submitted without the required proof of use for result-based claims
  • Requests made after the eligible return window has passed without prior contact
  • Situations where incorrect address information was provided at checkout and delivery failed
  • Purchases made under a promotional offer that explicitly stated no refunds at the time of purchase

If you are unsure whether your situation is eligible, please reach out before assuming the answer is no. We review every case individually and with good faith.

7. Tips to Avoid Issues

A smooth experience starts before you place your order. Here are our top recommendations:

  • Check sizing charts: Before ordering apparel or equipment, always consult the size guide. Sizing varies between suppliers.
  • Double-check your delivery address: Verify your full address — street, number, city, country, and postal code — before confirming any order.
  • Keep your confirmation email: Your order number is in your confirmation email. Save it — you will need it for any support request.
  • Start your program promptly: Digital programs are best started right away to maximize your access window and track your progress from day one.
  • Track your progress from the start: If you want the option of a result-based refund, documenting your journey from the beginning makes the process smoother.
  • Contact us early: If something feels off — with delivery, access, or results — reach out sooner rather than later. Early contact gives us more options to help you.
